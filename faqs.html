Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common queries about EduNex courses, billing, accounts, and technical issues.

General Questions

What is EduNex?

EduNex is an online learning platform offering courses, mentorship programs, and live sessions to help learners grow professionally.

Who can use EduNex?

Anyone — from students and professionals to hobbyists — can join EduNex and start learning instantly.

Is EduNex free to use?

EduNex offers both free and premium content. You can explore free lessons before enrolling in paid courses.

Courses & Learning

How are EduNex courses structured?

Courses are divided into modules, each containing video lectures, notes, and quizzes for interactive learning.

Can I access courses after completion?

Yes. Once you purchase a course, you have lifetime access to it — including all updates.

Do I get a certificate?

Yes. Upon completing a course, EduNex provides a digital certificate that can be shared on LinkedIn or added to your résumé.

Account & Profile

How can I edit my profile?

Go to your Dashboard → Profile → Edit. You can update your name, photo, and other details anytime.

I forgot my password. What do I do?

Use the “Forgot Password” option on the login page and follow the email reset instructions.

Can I delete my EduNex account?

Yes. Contact our support team at support@edunex.com to permanently delete your account and data.

Payments & Refunds

What payment methods do you support?

We support UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, PayPal, and Net Banking for secure and fast payments.

Do you offer refunds?

Refunds are available within 7 days of purchase if the course was not accessed or has technical issues.

Can I get an invoice?

Yes. After every successful payment, an invoice is automatically emailed to your registered address.

Technical Support

The video player isn't working — what should I do?

Try clearing your browser cache or switching to a different browser. If the issue continues, contact support@edunex.com.

I’m unable to access my course.

Ensure you’re logged into the correct account. If still inaccessible, send your order ID to our support team for help.

Do you have a mobile app?

Yes, EduNex mobile app is launching soon on Play Store and App Store for seamless on-the-go learning.
