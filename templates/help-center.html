How can we help you?

Welcome to the EdTube Help Center. Browse through common queries, troubleshooting tips, and platform guidance. If you can’t find what you’re looking for, our support team is always ready to assist.

Account & Login

How do I create a new EdTube account?

Click the “Sign Up” button on our homepage and complete the registration form using your email or Google account.

I forgot my password — what should I do?

Click “Forgot Password” on the login page and follow the instructions to reset it securely through your email.

Can I change my registered email ID?

Yes. Go to Account Settings → Profile → Change Email. You’ll receive a verification link on your new address.

Courses & Learning

How do I enroll in a new course?

From your dashboard, click “Browse Courses,” select your desired topic, and press “Enroll.”

Can I access my courses offline?

Offline access is currently in development, but you can download notes and resources in PDF format.

Are live sessions recorded?

Yes. All live classes are recorded and uploaded to your course library within 24 hours.

Technical Support

The website is not loading properly — what should I do?

Clear your browser cache and try again. If the issue persists, contact edtube.edu@gmail.com.

Video playback is buffering or lagging.

Try switching video quality or using a stable Wi-Fi connection. You can also use our mobile app for smoother streaming.

Payments & Billing

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept UPI, debit/credit cards, PayPal, and Net Banking. EMI options are available for selected courses.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are applicable within 7 days of purchase for incomplete or faulty course access. Terms apply.

General Help

How do I contact my instructor?

Navigate to your enrolled course → Instructor tab → “Message Instructor.”

Can I suggest a new feature or improvement?

Absolutely! Use our Report an Issue form or email edtube.edu@gmail.com.

Need more help?

Our support team is available Monday–Saturday, 9 AM to 6 PM IST.
