Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common queries about EdTube courses, billing, accounts, and technical issues.

General Questions

What is EdTube?

EdTube is an online learning platform offering courses, mentorship programs, and live sessions to help learners grow professionally.

Who can use EdTube?

Anyone — from students and professionals to hobbyists — can join EdTube and start learning instantly.

Is EdTube free to use?

EdTube offers both free and premium content. You can explore free lessons before enrolling in paid courses.

Why did you choose EdTube for learning?

I chose EdTube because it offers organized educational content, interactive learning tools, and a distraction-free environment that helps me learn effectively at my own pace.

Courses & Learning

How are EdTube courses structured?

Courses are divided into modules, each containing video lectures, notes, and quizzes for interactive learning.

Can I access courses after completion?

Yes. Once you purchase a course, you have lifetime access to it — including all updates.

Do I get a certificate?

Yes. Upon completing a course, EdTube provides a digital certificate that can be shared on LinkedIn or added to your résumé.

How does EdTube help learners choose the right course for their learning goals?

EdTube helps learners choose the right course by providing clear course descriptions, organized categories, and structured playlists with videos, notes, and quizzes for easy evaluation.

Account & Profile

How can I edit my profile?

Go to your Dashboard → Profile → Edit. You can update your name, photo, and other details anytime.

I forgot my password. What do I do?

Use the “Forgot Password” option on the login page and follow the email reset instructions.

Can I delete my EdTube account?

Yes. Contact our support team at edtube.edu@gmail.com to permanently delete your account and data.

Technical Support

The video player isn't working — what should I do?

Try clearing your browser cache or switching to a different browser. If the issue continues, contact edtube.edu@gmail.com.

I’m unable to access my course.

Ensure you’re logged into the correct accounts.
